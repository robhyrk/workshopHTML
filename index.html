Delivery Services

Questions? We have answers!

  1. What happens if my package is lost?
    2. A: Our delivery system sends a beacon to other porters in our network to notify them of lost packages that they can pick up and continue the delivery.
  2. How do I know my porter is not a raider in disguise?
    3. A: All of our porters carry ID badges that our biometrically linked. Before answering any deliveries please ensure you scan their ID badge.
  3. I think my porter was captured by raiders. What can I do?
    4. A: Unfortunately raiders have become prominent in certain delivery areas. If you think your porter has been captured please contact us and we will send a team to have them rescued.
